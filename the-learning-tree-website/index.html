About Us

Our Story

The Learning Tree was established in 2015 by Kevin and Susan Cross. What started with just 5 children in their home has grown into a trusted pillar of the Minot community.

We started with 5 kids in our home, we soon grew out of our house and into The Learning Tree at Oak Park and in July 2021 we opened The Learning Tree at Little Flower. We feel so fortunate to provide a much needed service to the Minot area and have met so many wonderful families and life long friends along the way.

Our Mission

The Learning Tree exists to provide a safe, developmentally appropriate environment for children ages 6 weeks to 12 years old. Our focus is to provide a stimulating early care and education experience, which promotes each child's social/emotional, physical and cognitive development. Our goal is to support children's desire to be life-long learners.

Our Programs

Growing Together at Every Stage

0-17 Months

Infant Room

Our infant room provides a nurturing environment where your baby will discover new sounds, foods, and experiences. Teachers work with parents to establish feeding and sleep schedules while tracking developmental milestones.

Daily activities include: Art, tummy time, outdoor walks (weather permitting)

$510 / 2 weeks
18-36 Months

Toddler Room

Designed for crawlers and new walkers, our toddler program helps children transition from bottles to cups and cribs to sleeping mats. We work closely with parents on introducing new foods.

Daily activities include: Sensory play, art projects, music and movement

$495 / 2 weeks
3 Year Olds

Preschool

Our preschool program gives children their first exposure to a school setting. We practice social interactions, learn routines, and improve skills like using art tools and name writing.

Requirements: Children must be potty-trained and use the bathroom independently.

$480 / 2 weeks
4-5+ Years

Pre-Kindergarten

Preparing children for kindergarten success through reading, writing, math, and science. We focus on fine motor skills, scissor skills, and letter recognition while building confidence.

Requirements: Children must be potty-trained and use the bathroom independently.

$460-470 / 2 weeks

We accept Child Care Assistance (CCAP). A 2-week deposit is required for all families. Contact us for enrollment details.
